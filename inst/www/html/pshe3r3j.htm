Power Graphs for Survival Studies

This window is used to produce plots of power vs. alternative hypotheses or power vs. sample size for survival studies. The alternative hypotheses may be expressed either as median experimental survival times or as relative risks (this choice is determined in the Design section of the main PS window). The variable that is not chosen for the x axis is held constant for each sample size curve. You may plot multiple curves on the same graph with different values of this third variable.
