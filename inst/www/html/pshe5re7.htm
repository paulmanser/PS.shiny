Power Graphs for Linear Regression (2 Treatment)

This window is used to produce plots of power vs. alternative hypotheses or power vs. sample size for studies that compare two simple linear regressions. The alternative hypotheses may be expressed as the difference in slops of two independent simple linear regression curves, or the difference in y-intercepts of these curves. The variable that is not chosen for the x axis is held constant for each sample size curve. You may plot multiple curves on the same graph with different values of this third variable.
