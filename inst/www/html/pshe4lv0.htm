Survival Overview

Survival Studies -- Evaluating independent cohorts using the log-rank test. The approach of Schoenfeld and Richter (1982) is used. The user may specify the ratio of the number of patients in the cohorts being compared. In survival studies the response from each patient consists of a dichotomous fate variable and a follow-up interval. The fate variable indicates the patient's status at the end of her follow-up.

The Survival dialog box has four sections: Output, Design, Input, and Description.

Each null hypothesis is tested with respect to a two-sided alternative hypothesis.
